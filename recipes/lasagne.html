Description

Is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

INGREDIENTS FOR THE GREEN EGG PUFF PASTRY:

  • Remilled durum wheat semolina 350g
  • Spinach 250g
  • 3 Yolks
  • "00" flour 150 g
  • 2 Eggs

FOR THE RAGU:

  • Beef (minced beef, coarse minced and mixed) 300g
  • Carrots 50g
  • Golden onions 50g
  • Tomato puree 300g
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1 tablespoon
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Bacon 150g
  • Celery 50g
  • Red wine 100g
  • Vegetable broth to taste
  • Fine salt to taste.
