Lasagne

Description

Is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

INGREDIENTS FOR THE GREEN EGG PUFF PASTRY:

  • Remilled durum wheat semolina 350g
  • Spinach 250g
  • 3 Yolks
  • "00" flour 150 g
  • 2 Eggs

FOR THE RAGU:

  • Beef (minced beef, coarse minced and mixed) 300g
  • Carrots 50g
  • Golden onions 50g
  • Tomato puree 300g
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1 tablespoon
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Bacon 150g
  • Celery 50g
  • Red wine 100g
  • Vegetable broth to taste
  • Fine salt to taste

FOR DRESSING

  • Butter to taste
  • Parmesan Cheese DOP 270g

Steps:

  1. To prepare lasagna Bolognese, start with the ragù sauce. Prepare the vegetable broth, which you'll need to keep warm. Then, take the pancetta and slice it into strips.
  2. With a knife or a mezzaluna, finely chop it.
  3. Separately, prepare a fine mince with carrots, onions, celery, and set it aside.
  4. In a saucepan, pour a drizzle of oil and add the pancetta. Break it up well with a spoon.
  5. and let it brown for a few minutes. Then, add the minced vegetables and let it flavor for 5-6 minutes.
  6. Then add the ground meat.
  7. Stir and increase the heat. Let the meat brown slowly; it should be well browned to seal in the juices and become tender, not mushy.
  8. Deglaze with the red wine.
  9. Then wait for it to completely evaporate and add the tomato passata.
  10. Stir; the ragù should cook for two hours. When it starts boiling again, you can add a little hot broth, one or two ladlefuls.
  11. Then let the ragù simmer for at least a couple of hours. You should cook it with the lid on, but not completely closed.
  12. Check and stir occasionally, adding more broth if needed, and let it cook for the indicated time. Now, let's move on to the pasta. First, pour the spinach into a pan.
  13. Add a little water, cover with a lid.
  14. and let them cook until wilted, it will take about 5-6 minutes in total.
  15. At this point, drain them, let them cool down, and squeeze them well.
  16. Transfer them to a blender.
